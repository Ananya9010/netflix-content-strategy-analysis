# netflix-content-strategy-analysis
The project analyzes patterns in Netflix’s content addition strategy and how it has evolved over time. The goal is to understand when, how, and what type of content Netflix adds to maximize user engagement.

## Objectives

* Analyze year-wise growth of Netflix content
* Identify monthly and seasonal trends
* Study Movie vs TV Show distribution over time
* Examine content addition lag (release year vs added year)
* Understand genre and country-based content strategy

## Key Insights

* Netflix content increased rapidly after 2015
* Peak additions occur during holiday seasons (Dec–Jan)
* Shift towards TV Shows, indicating binge-watching trends
* Reduced content lag suggests focus on newer and original content
* Increasing global content from countries beyond the US
